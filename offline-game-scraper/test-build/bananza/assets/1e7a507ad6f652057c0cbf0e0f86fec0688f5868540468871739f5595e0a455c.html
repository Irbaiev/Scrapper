Crash Games

This is a game of chance with dynamic odds, where players place bets and watch a multiplier increase in real time. The goal is to cash out before the game crashes to secure winnings.

Basic Info

The game interface provides key session information, including:

- BALANCE: The money or credits available in the player's casino account to wager.

- BET: The amount set as the current wager.

- WIN: The value earned in the last round.

- ROUND ID: A unique identifier for each game round, updated at the start of every round and displayed until the next game begins.

Game Rules

1. Betting Phase: Players have a set time (e.g., 10 seconds) to place their bets before the round begins.

2. Multiplier Growth: Once the round starts, the multiplier begins at 1.00x and increases progressively.

3. Cash Out: Players must manually cash out before the crash event to secure their winnings.

4. Crash Event: The round ends at a random crash point, and any players who have not cashed out lose their bets.

5. Payouts: The winnings are calculated as `cash-out multiplier × bet amount`.

Bet Settings

Players can adjust their bet amount within a predefined range using the controls in the betting section. Some crash games allow placing multiple simultaneous bets.

Auto Cash Out

Players can set a predefined multiplier at which the game will automatically cash out their bet, ensuring they lock in winnings without manual intervention.

Playing the Game

1. Set the desired bet amount and place the bet before the round starts.

2. Watch the multiplier increase in real time.

3. Click "Cash Out" at any moment before the crash to secure winnings.

4. If the multiplier crashes before cashing out, the bet is lost.

Autoplay

- A fixed number of rounds to play automatically.

- A specific cash-out multiplier.

- Conditions to stop autoplay based on total winnings or losses.

Other Controls

Players can adjust settings such as sound, speed, and graphics quality. A "Lobby" or "Home" button allows players to exit the game and return to the casino’s main menu.
